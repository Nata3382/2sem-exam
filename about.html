About me

Hi, I’m Arthur

My name is Arthur Monsacré, I am a 26y-o furniture maker originally from Paris, France. I created Monsi in June 2018. I first came to Copenhagen after my education in Paris in 2015, and had the chance to meet two fabulous woodworkers (Anders Guld & Kasper Løvenholdt) who took me under their wings for a couple of months. They could introduce me to Danish design and another approach regarding furniture making. After that, I went to Asia for about a year to perform my skills in different countries with woodworkers coming from a different background and use of machines than I could experience in Europe. I then went to the west part of France (Pays de la Loire), where I was making custom made terrasses in different shapes and heights. I could then have the opportunity to come back to Copenhagen and join the ‘Amager Snedkerhus’ collective in order to open my own company.

Custom made projects, indoor/outdoor furniture, arrangement of spaces and adapting to the lifestyle of my customers from designing solutions to fabrication/mounting are my main occupations. I have also been working with some artists and designers in order to bring to life wood-related ideas and art projects.

A focus on the wood

fsc

FSC certified wood

The wood used for my projects is labelled FSC. This label ensures that the wood comes from forests that are managed responsibly. The wood is also a reusable resource and its recycling process needs less energy in comparison to other materials such as steel or aluminium. The wood that is used is mostly German oak or Scandinavian pine, in order to limit the travel impact and be as eco-friendly as possible.
